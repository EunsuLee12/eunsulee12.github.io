Mechanics and Design

As a final project in the course Mechanics and Design, our team modeled an airplane wing to calculate the displacement and stress distribution when specific loads are applied. We used MATLAB, HyperMesh and analytic method to solve the problem, and compared the results of the three methods. Through this course, I learned the basics of Finite Element Method, and applied these by generating MATLAB code and using a commercial tool.
