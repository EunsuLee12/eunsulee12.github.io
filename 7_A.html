Odysseus Program

In my first year of high school, I participated in the Odysseus program, which was hosted by a local national university. It is a program that recruits excellent high school students and allows them to take part in engineering projects. I applied for the Aerospace Engineering department and built a controllable airship with students from different schools. This project was my first time working on an engineering project.
