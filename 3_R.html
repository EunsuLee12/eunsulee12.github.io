Robocon at SNU

Our team “Champion” won first place in the robot competition held at Seoul National University, which was held in the Creative Engineering Design course. As a reward, we were granted the opportunity to participate in the International Robocon the following year.
The main task of the contest was to pick up objects with different shapes and masses and deliver them to designated areas to score points. Our team made two robots: one for picking up the objects and one for delivering them to the area assigned to the highest point. I took this class in my freshman year; it was my first time designing and fabricating a robot using CAD and various machine tools.
