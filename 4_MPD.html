Mechanical Product Design

In Mechanical Product Design course, each team was required to design a device that can sort two types of balls: ping-pong balls and squash balls. The objective of the course was to incorporate systematic procedures into the overall design process. Our team’s device utilized the weight difference of the two types of balls by placing linear springs in the path where balls roll down. Although our team was unable to make the actual device due to COVID-19, I experienced the process of systematically designing a mechanical product that can perform given tasks.
